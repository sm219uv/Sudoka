Introduction

Sudoka ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Sudoka is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Progress: Your current game, best times, and statistics are stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Account Required: You can play the game without creating an account.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage feature. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your game data
  • Clearing your browser data will erase your game progress

Third-Party Services

Sudoka uses the following third-party library:

  • canvas-confetti: Used for celebration animations when you win. This library runs entirely in your browser and does not collect any data.

Internet Connection

Sudoka can work offline after the initial load. The app uses a Service Worker to cache game files, allowing you to play without an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Sudoka is a puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
